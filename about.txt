9:T587,<h1>About</h1>
<h2>Why</h2>
<p>This website is created with intention to inform Trustroots community members about changes that have been happening within the Trustroots organization.</p>
<p>It's also a way to direct feelings of anger, pain and frustration into something useful for others.</p>
<h2>Who</h2>
<ul>
<li>
<p><em>you</em> - If you want to contribute to this project, <a href="https://github.com/trustroots-community/trustrots/pulls">open a PR</a> or reach out via Matrix. You can also send your suggestions, and generally promote the project.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://trustroots.org/profile/mrkvon">mrkvon</a> has been a member of Trustroots since December 11 2014 as 33rd member of the network. They started volunteering <a href="https://github.com/Trustroots/trustroots/pull/361">in June 2016</a> as a developer. <a href="https://github.com/Trustroots/trustroots/commits/master/?author=mrkvon">See their code contributions</a>. They <a href="https://mrkvon.org/blog/leaving-trustroots">left volunteering</a> in January 2021 after a burnout. They came back in the summer 2022 to help shape a community-driven future. On January 25 2024 they were kicked out of Trustroots github organization along with many other volunteers, and banned from commenting. They are interested in intentional communities, and currently contribute to <a href="https://sleepy.bike">sleepy.bike</a>.</p>
</li>
